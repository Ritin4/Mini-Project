Having realized importance of information technology is improving operational efficiency; the department started its computerized efforts early on. The primary purpose of the software is maintaining information to monitor and enhance the performance of department.

The primary purpose of "GRIEVANCE HANDLING SYSTEM" is to develop, maintain a community information network which provides open and free access to online information for the citizens. The hallmark of this community information network is ability of general public to obtain information that may not have been previously or easily accessible to them. This pertains to maintains of details and grievances received action taken and status of the grievances.

The new system will provide all the details of the current municipality through online. With this system we can send the complaints, get the complaint status from the departments etc. In this system we have different modules like administrator section, department section, user section can maintain the all the details. This system will be developed efficiently and effectively with all the modules.
